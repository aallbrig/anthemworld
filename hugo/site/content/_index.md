        <h2>About Anthem World</h2>
        <p>National anthems are powerful symbols of national identity and pride. Each anthem tells a unique story about a country's history, values, and aspirations. Anthem World brings together all 193 UN-recognized countries' national anthems in one interactive experience.</p>
        
        <h3 class="mt-4">Features</h3>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Interactive World Map:</strong> Click any country to instantly view its anthem information</li>
            <li><strong>Audio Playback:</strong> Listen to each national anthem (coming soon)</li>
            <li><strong>Searchable Database:</strong> Find countries by name, anthem title, or other criteria</li>
            <li><strong>Comprehensive Information:</strong> Learn about composers, adoption dates, and historical context</li>
        </ul>
        
        <h3 class="mt-4">Data Sources</h3>
        <p>Our data comes from trusted open sources including:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>REST Countries API for country information</li>
            <li>Wikidata for anthem metadata</li>
            <li>Wikimedia Commons for audio recordings</li>
        </ul>
